Mahatma Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

Father of India

Studio photograph of Gandhi, 1931

Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi life:

  • 1869 - Birth at Porbunder in Gujarat (Sudamapuri, Kathiawad)
  • 1876- Beginning of Education at Rajkot
  • 1888- Left for London to study Law
  • 1891- Passed the Law examination
  • 1891- In India, introduced to Raychandbhai (Whom Gandhiji regarded as his Guru)
  • 1893- At Pietermaritzberg station Gandhiji was ordered to go into the van compartment of the train although he held a first class ticket. On his refusal, a constable was brought and he was forcibly ejected, his bundles pitched out after him. He was left to shiver in the waiting room all night.
  • 1894- Founded Natal Indian Congress to fight colour prejudice
  • 1901- Moved a resolution on South Africa at Calcutta Congress session.
  • 1903- Founded Transvaal British India Association
  • 1917- Champaran Satyagraha
  • 1924- Started 21 days fast for Hindu-Muslim unity
  • 1930- Arrested and taken to Yervada jail
  • 1948- Started fast in Delhi against communal riots
  • 1948- The day of martyrdom, was shot dead while on his way to evening prayer ground at Birla House (now Gandhi Smriti) by Nathuram Vinayak Godse.
